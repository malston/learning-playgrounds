6L-GThe Six-Level GenAI Governance framework was developed by Engin Bozdag and Stefano Bennati in AI Governance (Manning, 2026). It adapts the Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) cycle from ISO/IEC 42001 and the NIST AI Risk Management Framework, making it specific to generative AI with six named checkpoints and concrete controls at each level.
